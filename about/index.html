How

These pages are hosted as a static site on Github, the static content which forms the pages, is generated with a Go tool called Hugo.

What

On these pages you’ll find some short posts about things/projects/info, which I have taken notes to remember. These pages are for me to recall, how I have done a task and are as such my “Note Book”. I happen to share them, as I hope someone else may find them useful.

Where possible, I do try to give credit by references if they where useful.

Why

Mainly just for my recollection and sometimes, I like to write some stuff.

When

There are no schedules. I make no promises..

C’est la vie.
